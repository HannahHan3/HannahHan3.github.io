I'm Chinese Mongols, an Ethnic minorities in China. My hometown is a beautiful town on the border of Inner Mongolia province and Mongolia. People in the town are kind, and we speak mongolian in daily life. I left my hometown at the age of 14 to get a better education. I enjoy the time when I get back to my hometown and spend time with my parents, I'm their little girl forever.



I started playing the piano when I was 8 years old. Playing the piano is the first thing that comes to my mind when I want to enjoy the time alone and relax. I'm not a big fan of classical music, classical music is absolutely great, yet, I prefer some light and simple melody, which makes me feel relaxed and happy.

I learned swimming at Recreation Center in University of Maryland. I love it but I'm quite new at this.
